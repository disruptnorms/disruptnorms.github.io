Disrupting Academic Norms

Welcome to a space where sociology meets rebellion, where academic rigor collides with punk attitude. I'm dedicated to challenging conventional thinking, dismantling social barriers, and bringing fresh perspectives to the study of society.

My Mission

Through research, content creation, and community engagement, I explore the intersections of power, identity, and social change. My work focuses on making sociology accessible, relevant, and revolutionary for a new generation of thinkers and activists.

Why "Disrupt Norms"?

Because norms aren't neutral. They're constructed, maintained, and can be deconstructed. My approach combines rigorous sociological analysis with a commitment to social justice and a refusal to accept "that's just how things are" as an answer.
